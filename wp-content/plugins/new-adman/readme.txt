== Description ==

Lets you insert your ad-code (like AdSense) directly before, after or in the middle of your post content
or wherever you want without having to edit your template.

You can specify an ad-code to appear before and after your actual post content and one to appear in the middle of it.

Just install it, then go to Options -> New Adman. There you can set your ad-codes. The one to appear before your
post content will appear before your content, the one to appear in the middle will be calculated to appear
right in the middle of your posts (between tag groups, like paragraphs or divs), or where you inserted <!-- adman --> in it.

You can show or hide ad-codes for individual post on the post edition page.

New Adman based on the original Adman plugin than is no longer supported.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip into your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Options -> New Adman and set your ad-codes
